Our Ethos

Founded by a Green Beret, the spirit of this company reflects the values forged in discipline and acomplishment of the mission thinking. Integrity, brutally honest feedback, and placing the collective above the individual are IAG's operational principles.

We are not here merely to make vast riches. We are preparing for the realities of a world defined by scarcity of water, food and freedom. Our focus is self-reliance and self-sustainability. Not token gestures like paper straws, but real systems that can endure and adapt. Survival is not a lifestyle brand. It's the standard.
